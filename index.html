ESCAPE INTO NATURE

Riverview Spa & Bed and Breakfast - A Sanctuary of Serenity

Let the tranquil waters and natural beauty of Riverview Spa sweep you off your feet and soothe away the cares of everyday life. With breathtaking views that transform even the busiest of days into moments of peace, Riverview offers more than a destination—it offers a rejuvenating escape.

Nestled in the heart of the Kromdraai Conservation Area and overlooking the Zwartkop Mountains and surrounding hills, Riverview Spa is uniquely positioned just meters from the Crocodile River. Here, guests are immersed in untouched natural beauty, abundant birdlife, and a calm atmosphere we’ve proudly become known for.

As the saying goes, “Difficult roads often lead to beautiful destinations.” En route to Riverview, a scenic drive through the Cradle Moon Conservancy may offer sightings of giraffes, zebras, and impalas—setting the tone for a one-of-a-kind wellness experience.

Conveniently located less than 30 minutes from Johannesburg, 20 minutes from Lanseria, and an hour’s drive from Pretoria, Riverview is the perfect retreat for couples, families, or friends seeking a peaceful getaway. Whether you’re indulging in couples’ treatments, sharing a riverside picnic, or reconnecting during quality mother-daughter time, our spa is designed to help you unwind and reconnect.

Accommodation

Our seven private and comfortable chalets provide an ideal option for those looking to stay overnight after a day of relaxation. For groups, our self-catering thatched villa—accommodating up to eight guests—is perfect for weekend escapes and memory-making around the bonfire.

Being centrally located among Muldersdrift’s most popular wedding venues, Riverview is also a convenient and charming stayover for wedding guests, complete with a hearty breakfast the following morning.

Conferencing & Corporate Retreats

Our newest addition is a fully equipped boardroom, ideal for intimate team-building sessions, strategic retreats, and small conferences of up to 16 delegates. We also partner with neighbouring establishments to offer exciting outdoor activities such as game drives, quad biking, and horse riding.

Celebrating 21 Years of Wellness

This August marks Riverview Spa’s 21st anniversary—a milestone that reflects our unwavering commitment to authentic, nature-inspired hospitality. Over the years, our guests have come to value our grounded, heartfelt service, where nature provides the ambiance and elegance.

Our greatest achievement lies in our people. With a combined 140 years of service among our core team of 10 dedicated staff, Riverview Spa offers a warmth and consistency that makes guests feel right at home.

Our treatments are of an exceptional standard, our meals are wholesome and nourishing, and our hospitality is sincere. It’s this combination that makes Riverview truly different—an experience, not just a visit.
